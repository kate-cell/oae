Amusement Parks in Dubai

The best amusement parks in Dubai

The best amusement parks in Dubai are indoor and beach complexes with attractions, large and children's water parks, theme parks that are bright and amazing in their scope. In the material below - the most popular amusement parks in Dubai: with prices, photos and descriptions, reviews.

The main entertainment and attraction center in Dubai - Dubai Parks and Resorts - is located on 285 hectares in the northwestern part of the emirate - not far from the artificial island of Palm Jebel Ali.

It will take several days to visit most of Dubai Parks and Resorts attractions, as its territory is formed by large and distinctive theme parks - Motiongate, Bollywood, Legoland - and the Riverland Dubai resort area with an artificial river and boardwalk, shops, cafes and restaurants.

The most unusual outdoor activity center in Dubai is the Ski Dubai indoor ski complex, one of a kind in the Middle East and desert climate. There are slopes for skiers and snowboarders, a cable car. The show program at Ski Dubai is also more than original: daily, guests of the park become spectators of the march and penguin show.

When planning a trip to Dubai, most tourists dream of visiting the huge Ferrari theme park. It should be borne in mind that he is not in Dubai, but in the capital Abu Dhabi. Nevertheless, it is not difficult to get to it: by bus with a change in Abu Dhabi, with excursions (this destination is popular) or by taxi.

Amusement parks in Dubai

Dubai's largest indoor amusement park - IMG Worlds of Adventure. It is divided into thematic zones dedicated to the Cartoon Network cartoons, the Marvel universe, adventures and the lost world of dinosaurs.

With the love of Hollywood films and cartoons, the attractions of Motiongate Dubai Park were created, and in the Bollywood Parks Dubai theme park you will be able to feel the nostalgic atmosphere of Indian cinema - with indispensable Indian music and dancing, vibrant scenery and copies of attractions.

Legoland Dubai remains one of the most European amusement parks in Dubai, it pleases with a huge variety of all kinds of models from blocks (more than 15 thousand objects) and children's attractions.

Particularly memorable will be the rest in Dubai during the days of Global Village - the largest fair in the UAE, which is held in the amusement park "Dubailand".

Dubai Water Parks

Hot, arid weather reigns in Dubai for most of the year. Therefore, it is not surprising that water parks are so popular here, each of which is a real oasis of the sultry Emirates.

The largest water park in Dubai - Atlantis Aquaventure (Aquaventure Waterpark), is one of the objects of the five-star Atlantis The Palm Hotel and is located with it on the artificial island of Palm Jumeirah.

You can also have a great rest on the water slides in the large Wild Wadi Water Park located on the mainland, decorated in the style of Arabian tales.

The most unusual of the Dubai water parks is Legoland Water Park. The Lego corporate identity could not be avoided even on such a classic attraction as the Lazy River, all the rafts on which are decorated with soft large Lego blocks.
