Amusement Parks in Dubai

Park IMG Worlds of Adventure

How to get to Park

IMG Worlds of Adventure is a huge indoor theme park in Dubai, opened August 31, 2016. It is believed to be the largest indoor air-conditioned amusement park in the world. The closed cool territory of IMG Worlds of Adventure in Dubai is especially relevant for the hot climate of the United Arab Emirates, because there is an opportunity to relax and have fun at any time of the year.

The total floor space of the IMG Worlds of Adventure park is about 140,000 square meters and can accommodate up to 20,000 visitors per day. At the heart of the park are 4 “epic zones” of adventure - 2 zones are devoted to the products of the famous brands Cartoon Network and Marvel, as well as 2 own concepts from IMG - Boulevard IMG and Valley Lost.
