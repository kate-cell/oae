Amusement Parks in Dubai

Park Motiongate Dubai

How to get to Motiongate Dubai Park

Motiongate Dubai is one of Dubai Parks and Resorts' amusement parks dedicated to Hollywood films and cartoons. Its opening in Dubai took place on December 16, 2016. On the territory of Motiongate Dubai theme park there are several thematic zones with attractions and entertainments that colorfully and realistically transport visitors to the worlds of Kung Fu Pandas, Shrek, Madagascar, Smurfs, Ghosts and many others. Each of the zones represents film masterpieces created by the famous movie studios of America - Columbia Pictures, Lionsgate and DreamWorks Animation. A no less remarkable Smurf village was built next to them.
