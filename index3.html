Amusement Parks in Dubai

Bollywood Parks

How to get to Park

Bollywood Parks Dubai is the first theme park in the world dedicated to the world of Bollywood, with attractions, concerts and dances in the style of the best films of Indian cinema. Bollywood Parks was opened on November 17, 2016 in the territory of a large complex of amusement parks Dubai Parks and Resorts.

Next to Bollywood Parks Dubai there are Motiongate Dubai amusement parks with the world of Hollywood movies and cartoons, Legoland and a LEGO-style water park. All parks are located near the beautiful Riverland shopping and restaurant pedestrian area with an artificial river.

Prices in 2020

Prices for 1 day:

for adults - 175 dirhams,
for adult residents of the UAE - 99 dirhams,
children under 3 years old - free of charge.

1 day in 2 parks - 295 dirhams,

annual subscription to 2 parks (free parking and a discount in restaurants, shops and the hotel) - 475 dirhams,
annual subscription to 4 parks (free parking and a discount in restaurants, shops and the hotel) - 595 dirhams.
